Restore Your Mobility, Flexibility, and Quality of Life

Chronic inflammation can play a role in nearly every major disease, including heart disease, diabetes, cancer and Alzheimer’s. In fact, the World Health Organization says that chronic inflammatory conditions are the greatest health threat to humans.1 Acute inflammation is normal and healthy, but lingering chronic inflammation can be damaging. So why not do everything possible to bolster your immune system and lower unchecked inflammation?

Don’t let stiff joints and limited mobility hold you back. Restore your body’s vitality and get moving!

In a double-blind, placebo-controlled clinical study, Nopalea was shown to:

  • Improve joint mobility and range of motion
  • Improve neck and back motion
  • Increase mobility and flexibility
  • Reduce inflammation
  • Lower elevated at risk levels of C-reactive protein
  • Enhance quality of life
